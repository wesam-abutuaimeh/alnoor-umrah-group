The Saudi electronic visa

Saudi Arabia Online visa (E-VISA) enables travelers to get a multiple entry visa to visit Saudi for different reasons. It is done for you online so there is no need for you to visit any Saudi embassy or consulate and is delivered to you within 24 hours. The visa has multiple entries and is valid for one year from the date of issue. This visa has multiple uses such as visiting family, attending events, tourism, and Umrah but CANNOT be used for Hajj. The visa is valid for up to four entries to Saudi Arabia and allows you to stay up to 90 days upon each entry. The visa comes with mandatory insurance, which is a compulsory requirement in order to issue the visa.

electronic visa for saudi arabia requirements

Visa validity:

  • Valid for 1 year from issuing date .
  • Valid for up to 4 different entries .
  • Up to 90 days stay every entry.

Saudi Arabia Entry Rules:

  • MUST have a passport valid for six months from the date of intended travel.
  • You CANNOT overstay the 90 day periods.
  • The visa is not valid for work.
  • Must follow the countries Islamic guidelines.
  • No drugs.

Visa fees

  • One time payment of $230 that will cover the cost of the visa and the insurance which is mandated by Saudi government
